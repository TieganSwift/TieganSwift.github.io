Project name: Physical Intelligence


Prototype description:
Prototype will be presented a simple 3D object complete with wire frame weight and rigging. Object will be placed in a non-randomly generated environment provided by the use of the physics engine Unity to mimic real world forces. Through a deep learning algorithm, the 3D object will dynamically learn the most effective way to move with only the given attributes of the object.
Optimal movements may be determined by gauging most distance traveled in least amount of time or most distance traveled with the least amount of movement made by the object. Once optimization plateaus movement will be stored in a database so prototype may retain a muscle memory.
Implementation of this prototype will demonstrate my knowledge and application of deep learning algorithm. It will also display my problem solving ability to translate movement into a document format that can be drawn on to facilitate further deep learning when presented different, or even more complex 3D objects.
